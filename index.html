ABOUT

Led by producer, engineer, songwriter, and musician George Emmanuel, Pentagram Music Studio specializes in songwriting and production assistance, recording, mixing, and mastering for Extreme Metal, Rock, and Heavy Music.

Drawing from over 15 years of experience in music production and performance, the studio has contributed to projects involving artists such as Rotting Christ, Septicflesh, Thy Catafalque, Orphaned Land, Corvus Corax, Lacrimas Profundere, Lucifer's Child, and many more, as well as releases associated with labels and companies including Nuclear Blast, Metal Blade, Season of Mist, and Netflix productions.

Whether you are seeking creative guidance, professional recording, mixing, or mastering, our mission is simple: to help bring your artistic vision to life and realize the full potential of your music.

SERVICES

Songwriting & Production Assistance

Sometimes the difference between a good song and a great song lies in the details. By working closely with artists throughout the creative process, George Emmanuel helps refine arrangements, strengthen compositions, develop melodies, improve transitions, and maximize the impact of every song while remaining faithful to the band's vision and identity. From underground DIY releases to major-label productions, years of experience have helped countless artists achieve their creative goals and realize the full potential of their songs.

Recording

Every great production begins with a great performance. Work directly with George Emmanuel and benefit from years of experience as a producer, engineer, and professional touring musician. From capturing powerful drum performances to achieving inspiring guitar tones and expressive vocals, every recording session is focused on bringing out the strongest version of your music. Combining professional equipment, technical expertise, and a deep understanding of heavy music, Pentagram Music Studio helps transform ideas into recordings that are ready to stand alongside international releases.

Mixing

A great mix is where vision, emotion, and technical precision come together. At Pentagram Music Studio, every mix is approached with the goal of enhancing the identity and impact of your music while maintaining the character that makes it unique. Through careful balance, depth, clarity, and dynamics, each element is shaped to create a powerful and immersive listening experience. Whether you are aiming for modern aggression, organic warmth, or atmospheric depth, every project receives the attention and detail required to achieve a professional, release-ready result that translates across all listening environments.

Mastering

Every project has its own character, and every master should serve the artistic vision behind it. At Pentagram Music Studio, mastering is tailored to the specific needs of each release, ensuring optimal translation across all playback systems and formats. From warm, dynamic analog reel-to-reel tape mastering to modern digital masters with precision, clarity, and impact, every project receives an approach that complements its identity. The goal is not to force a particular sound, but to deliver a final master that faithfully represents your music and enhances the listening experience while remaining true to your vision.
